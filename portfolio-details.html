Project information

  • Project Title: Sunny Sips Business
  • Type of Project: Branding Design
  • Client/Target Audience: Sunny Sips – a startup smoothie business that uses fresh fruits and targets young adults and health-conscious individuals looking for refreshing, natural beverages
  • Purpose: To create a cohesive and modern brand identity for a product launch, including logo and promotional poster design
  • Tools used: Canva

Description

Sunny Sips is a conceptual smoothie brand developed using a ChatGPT prompt to explore creative brand identity design. Targeting young, health-conscious consumers, the project features a fresh and vibrant visual style. Using Canva, I designed a playful logo and promotional poster that highlight the brand’s natural ingredients and energetic appeal.
